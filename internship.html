About the Internship

During my internship at Digital Justice, which took place from 2023 Jan to 2023 Oct, I had the privilege of serving as a Web Developer. This experience allowed me to further develop my skills in web development and make meaningful contributions to the team.

Key Responsibilities

  • Collaborating with the development team to create and maintain web applications.
  • Assisting in the design and implementation of user-friendly, responsive web interfaces.
  • Debugging and troubleshooting issues to enhance website performance.
  • Embracing new technologies and coding practices to drive innovation and efficiency.

Skills Gained

  • Front-end development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks such as React and Angular.
  • Back-end development: Node.js, Express, and database management (SQL, NoSQL).
  • Version control systems, with a focus on Git and GitHub.
  • Debugging and problem-solving to optimize web applications.
